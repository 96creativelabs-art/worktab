Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how WorkTab protects your data and respects your privacy.

Last updated: December 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

WorkTab is designed with privacy in mind. We believe that your browsing data should remain private and under your control. This privacy policy explains how WorkTab handles your data and what information we collect, if any.

Data Collection

What We Collect

Local Storage Data

WorkTab stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Workspace configurations (names, colors, tab lists)
  • Tab titles and URLs for organization purposes
  • User preferences and settings
  • Session backup data
  • Analytics data (visit counts, session duration)

What We DON'T Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, address)
  • Browsing history outside of WorkTab
  • Content of web pages
  • Passwords or login credentials
  • Any data transmitted to external servers

Browser Permissions

Required Permissions

WorkTab requires certain browser permissions to function properly. Here's what each permission is used for:

Tabs Permission

Allows WorkTab to read tab information (title, URL, favicon) for workspace organization. We do not access tab content or personal data.

Storage Permission

Enables saving workspace configurations and user preferences locally on your device.

Context Menus Permission

Adds "Add to Workspace" options to right-click menus for easy tab organization.

Scripting Permission

Allows WorkTab to inject UI elements (modals, quick switcher) into web pages for enhanced functionality.

Analytics and Usage Data

Local Analytics Only

WorkTab includes analytics features to help you understand your browsing patterns. All analytics data is processed and stored locally:

Analytics Data Collected

  • Website visit counts and frequency
  • Session duration and timing
  • Tab activity patterns
  • Workspace usage statistics
  • Most visited domains

Privacy-First Analytics

  • No personal identification
  • No external data transmission
  • Data stays on your device
  • You can disable analytics in settings
  • Data can be cleared at any time

Your Data Control

Complete Control

You have complete control over your WorkTab data:

Export Your Data

Use the Backup & Restore feature to export all your workspaces, settings, and analytics data as a JSON file.

Clear Your Data

You can clear all WorkTab data at any time through the extension settings or by uninstalling the extension.

Disable Features

Turn off analytics, auto-save, or any other features you don't want to use through the settings panel.
